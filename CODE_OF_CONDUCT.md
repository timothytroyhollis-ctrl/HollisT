# Personal Contributor Code of Conduct

## My Pledge
I pledge to help make participation in open-source and technical communities a harassment-free, positive experience for everyone. This includes people of all ages, body sizes, visible or invisible disabilities, ethnicities, sex characteristics, gender identities and expressions, levels of experience, education, socio-economic backgrounds, nationalities, personal appearances, races, religions, and sexual identities or orientations.

I commit to acting in ways that support an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

## My Standards

### Behaviors I Strive For
- Showing empathy, patience, and kindness toward others
- Respecting differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
- Offering constructive feedback and receiving it gracefully
- Taking responsibility for mistakes and learning from them
- Prioritizing what benefits the broader community, not just myself

### Behaviors I Will Not Engage In
- Using sexualized language or imagery, or making sexual advances
- Trolling, insulting comments, personal attacks, or political attacks
- Harassment in public or private spaces
- Sharing someone’s private information without explicit permission
- Any conduct that would reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional or collaborative environment

## Scope
This personal Code of Conduct applies to my participation in all online and offline community spaces, including GitHub repositories, issues, pull requests, discussions, social media interactions related to my work, and any events where I represent myself as a contributor.

**Timothy.troy.hollis@gmail.com**

## Enforcement Guidelines
If I were ever to violate this Code of Conduct, I understand that the following actions may be taken by community maintainers or project owners. These guidelines describe potential outcomes, not expectations of my behavior.

### 1. Correction
**Community Impact:** Minor inappropriate language or behavior.  
**Possible Consequence:** A private, written communication providing clarity about the issue and why the behavior was inappropriate. A public apology may be requested.

### 2. Warning
**Community Impact:** A single or repeated violation.  
**Possible Consequence:** A formal warning outlining expectations for future behavior. I may be asked to avoid further interaction with affected individuals or maintainers for a specified period.

### 3. Temporary Ban
**Community Impact:** Serious or sustained inappropriate behavior.  
**Possible Consequence:** A temporary restriction from participating in community spaces or discussions for a defined period.

### 4. Permanent Ban
**Community Impact:** A pattern of harmful behavior, harassment, or aggression.  
**Possible Consequence:** A permanent removal from participation in the community or project.
